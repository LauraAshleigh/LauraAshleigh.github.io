MYOTHERAPY

A Myotherapist is a degree qualified 'hands-on' therapist. We use a variety of skills and techniques to treat muscles, joints and nerves. A session with a 'Myo' consists of a thorough assessment, which directs the choice of techniques used for treatment. A session with a Myo can assist injury recovery, sports recovery or relieve tired, sore and overworked structures.

Conditions we treat:
  • Back and neck pain
  • Limb pain and injury
  • Nerve pain
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Osteo arthritis
  • Ligament and tendon injury
  • Muscle aches, strains and soreness
  • Acute, sub-acute and chronic pain conditions
  • C-section, hysterectomy and abdominal scar immersion
Techniques we use:
  • Soft tissue massage
  • Myofascial release
  • Myofascial cupping
  • Dry and electro needling
  • Nerve manipulation and mobilisation
  • Join mobilisation
  • Muscle energy technique
  • Corrective exercise
  • Scar immersion
